\subsection*{Big O notation}

$ f, g \in \mathbb{N}$, $f = O(g)$ means that f grows no faster than g if $\exists c > 0$ s.t. $F(n) \leq c g(n)$ 

$ f = \Theta(g)$ means $g = O(f)$

$f = \Theta(g)$ IFF $ f = O(g) \ \& \ g = \Theta(g)$
